Data Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your Hi!Book account and all associated personal data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Email us at hibook.support@gmail.com with the subject line: “Data Deletion Request — Hi!Book”.
  2. Include the following in your message:
    • Full name
    • Email address linked to your account
    • Username (if applicable)
    • Optional: reason for deletion
  3. We may verify your identity for security reasons — this may include confirming account details, sending a confirmation code, or other reasonable verification steps.

What Will Be Deleted

Once verified, we will permanently remove your account, profile data, and user content from active systems. Some technical logs or backups may persist temporarily for security or compliance purposes.

Timeline

We acknowledge deletion requests within 5 business days and complete the process within 30 days where feasible, unless legal obligations require retention.

Exceptions & Limitations

We may retain limited data as required by law, such as records for fraud prevention, tax, or dispute resolution. Anonymized and aggregated data may be used for analytics.
