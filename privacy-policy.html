Introduction

Hi!Book ("we", "our" or "us") operates the Hi!Book website and applications (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: name, email, phone number, username, profile info, date of birth, and other data you add to your profile.
  • User Content: posts, messages, comments, photos, videos, friend requests, gifts, live streams, and other content you create.
  • Payment Data: transaction and billing info. We do not store full card details — payments are processed by trusted third parties.
  • Technical Data: IP address, device type, browser, OS, cookies, and analytics data.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve the Service
  • Enable friend requests, messaging, gifting, and live streaming
  • Authenticate accounts and enhance security
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Process purchases and enforce our Terms
  • Communicate updates, offers, and alerts
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

3. Sharing Information

  • Other users: your public profile and shared content.
  • Service providers: companies that help operate Hi!Book (hosting, analytics, payment processing, etc.).
  • Legal reasons: when required by law or to protect rights and safety.
  • Business transfers: if Hi!Book merges, is acquired, or sells assets.

4. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data.
  • Control cookies through browser settings.
  • Adjust your privacy and communication preferences in settings.
  • Request account deletion by emailing us at hibook.support@gmail.com.

5. Data Security

We use appropriate security measures to protect your data, but no system is entirely secure. Please use strong passwords and be alert for phishing or suspicious links.

6. Children’s Privacy

Hi!Book is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If such data is discovered, it will be deleted promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Major updates will be communicated via the app or email. Continued use after updates means you accept the revised version.

8. Contact Us

For questions or concerns, contact us at hibook.support@gmail.com.
